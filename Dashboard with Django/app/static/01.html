Tamil Nadu tourist found dead in Kullu

MANALI
A tourist from Tamil Nadu, who had gone to the Kheerganga trek in Manikaran valley of Kullu on March 25, was found dead on Thursday. Police identified the body with the help of documents found with the deceased and brought the body to a Kullu hospital. On Friday, his family members reached Kullu and identified the body.

Kullu superintendent of police Shalini Agnihotri said the deceased has been identified as Manoj Rathinam, a resident of Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu. "He was studying in a college in Panipat. On March 25, he told his parents that he is going to Kheerganga on a trek," she said.

The parents do not suspect foul play but police are taking all possibilities into account. "The body has been sent to Mandi for forensic examination," the SP said.
